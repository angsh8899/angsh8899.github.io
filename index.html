AboutAngles · How / Why

⚑ World's Firsts. Innovative Generalism.

I am Khayyam, and I'm an Innovative Generalist. Which is to say: I've been pioneering frontier technology for a couple decades — with another couple decades of tuition paid at the Institute of Trial & Error. In other words, I've fallen more times than most others.

HOW /

All the angles. If you don't have all the angles covered, how is one to see all the sides to the problem?

A predisposition for storytelling, pattern recognition, and connoisseurship — with a penchant for repeating any process if it can be understood with a new lens. From a young age, immersing into environments and enveloping the long-tailed narrative that flows when the creative side and the technological chops sit in balance.

WHY /

Familiar, yet nostalgic. Translating complicated concepts into narratives that seem familiar, yet nostalgic in ways. Award-winning, in fact.

If you're working on a World's First — or need the full tool belt of a generalist — please get a hold of me. The time is now.
